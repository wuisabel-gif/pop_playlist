Profile

Ayesha Erotica started uploading to SoundCloud in the mid-2010s from Huntington Beach, California, fusing 2000s nostalgia, brash humour and a maximalist electro-pop sound that would later be filed under hyperpop. Working largely as a self-taught producer, she wrote, recorded and engineered her own catalog at home.

Her influence outran her fame: she co-wrote and produced for collaborators in the same scene and is widely credited as a formative reference point for artists such as Slayyyter, who first connected with her online. In 2018, after a doxxing incident leaked her personal information, she pulled most of her music offline and stepped away from the public eye.

She remains a polarising figure — her catalog leans deliberately explicit and satirical, and she faced criticism over offensive language in resurfaced early material. After years away she re-emerged in 2023, with fans continuing to preserve and circulate her scattered discography online.
