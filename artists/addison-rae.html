Profile

Addison Rae Easterling rose to fame in 2019–2020 as one of TikTok's most-followed creators, amassing tens of millions of followers before moving into acting and music. Her 2021 single “Obsessed” drew a mixed reception — but it also signalled a serious intent to make pop.

The turning point was her relationship with the genre's underground. Charli xcx mentored her and appeared on her 2023 debut EP AR, and Rae credits that period with reshaping her musical direction. The follow-up singles — “Diet Pepsi,” “Aquamarine,” “High Fashion” — earned genuine critical momentum.

Her 2025 debut album Addison, made almost entirely with producers Luka Kloser and Elvira Anderfjärd and inspired in part by Madonna's Ray of Light, was framed as “the first & last album by Addison Rae” — a clean statement of a new identity built on dance, mood and movement.
