Profile

Alice Longyu Gao is a Chinese-born singer, songwriter, DJ and performance artist who moved to the United States at 17, studied philosophy at Boston University, and is now based between New York City and Los Angeles. She built a cult following in the hyperpop underground with confrontational, hyper-online songs and a deliberately over-the-top persona.

Her sound is brash, saccharine and abrasive at once: blown-out synths, sugar-rush vocals and tongue-in-cheek bravado. Breakout single “Dumb Bitch Juice” (2019) was produced by frequent collaborator Dylan Brady of 100 gecs, and she teamed with Alice Glass on the 2021 single “LEGEND.” Across the EP High Dragon and Universe and later releases she leans into a maximalist, queer, anything-goes aesthetic.
