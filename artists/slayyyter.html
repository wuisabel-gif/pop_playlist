Profile

Slayyyter began making music on her phone in high school around Kirkwood, Missouri, then started uploading to SoundCloud in college. Her 2018 debut single “BFF” featured Ayesha Erotica, whom she'd contacted as a fan, and the two became frequent collaborators across her early work.

The viral success of tracks like “Mine” pulled her out of the pop underground and led to her 2019 self-titled mixtape. She refined the sound across Troubled Paradise (2021) and the '80s-leaning Starfucker (2023), which became her first entry on a Billboard chart. Her single “Daddy AF” later surfaced in the films Bodies Bodies Bodies and Anora.

Now based in Brooklyn and signed to Columbia, she's toured alongside Charli xcx, Kesha and Tove Lo, building a reputation for bratty, retro-futurist pop with a sharp sense of humour about fame itself.
