# SUGARCRASH//

A love letter to the pop and hyperpop artists I can't stop playing.

I wanted a single place that felt like the music does — loud, candy-coated, a little broken in the best way — instead of a flat list of names. So this is part fan zine, part fake web-radio: a glittering, glitchy rotation of the artists on heavy repeat, from Ayesha Erotica and Slayyyter to Charli xcx, Lady Gaga, Doja Cat and beyond. Each one gets a card, a story about why they're here, and a real Spotify player so you can actually hit play.

It's a fan project, made for fun — a way to celebrate this corner of pop and share the rotation with whoever wanders in.
